## 🤖 Apply AI: Analyze Customer Reviews

**Issued By:** Cisco Networking Academy  
**Completion Date:** July 06, 2026

### 📖 Course Overview

This course focused on applying Artificial Intelligence to analyze customer reviews and extract meaningful business insights. Through hands-on activities, I learned how AI can automate text analysis, identify customer sentiments, classify topics, remove redundant data, and generate actionable recommendations for improving products and services.
